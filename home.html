Client Questions & Implementation Plan

The questions below willl help us assess what you want to acheive with us.

Questions for Client:

  1. What is the primary goal of your website?
  2. Who is your target audience?
  3. Do you have any specific design preferences or branding guidelines?
  4. Will the website require e-commerce functionality?
  5. How many pages do you anticipate the website having?
  6. Do you need any special features or integrations (e.g., contact forms, social media integration)?
  7. Do you have any existing content (text, images) for the website, or will we need to create it?
  8. Are there any websites you admire or competitors' sites you'd like to emulate or differentiate from?
  9. What is your desired timeline for the website launch?
  10. What is your budget for website development and ongoing maintenance?

This is our usual Implementation plan that most of our clients follow when they want to build their package with us.
